This document is from IBK Pension Insurance.

Document structure
- Use the printed document date in the "기준일자" field as base_date.
- Do not use the email Date header as base_date for this counterparty.
- Extract only rows whose "운용사" value contains "삼성".
- The key columns are:
  - "펀드명"
  - "운용사"
  - "정산액"
  - "예정 정산액 기준일+1"
  - "예정 정산액 기준일+2"
  - "예정 정산액 기준일+3"
  - "펀드코드"

Transaction rules
- Treat "정산액" as one same-day T0 net slot for that row.
- Treat "예정 정산액 기준일+1", "예정 정산액 기준일+2", and "예정 정산액 기준일+3" as future net slots for T+1, T+2, and T+3 respectively.
- Use only the net amount columns "정산액" and "예정 정산액 기준일+N" as order evidence for this format.
- Do not emit any separate T0 or future SUB/RED orders from side columns such as "설정액" and "해지액", even when "정산액" is blank.
- Determine order_type from the sign of the selected net amount:
  - negative = RED
  - positive = SUB
- Classify "정산액" as CONFIRMED internally.
- Classify every "예정 정산액 기준일+N" slot as PENDING internally.
- Ignore any row whose "정산액" and every visible future scheduled amount are 0, blank, or "-".

Field handling
- Use the printed "펀드코드" and "펀드명" values as they appear in the document.
- Preserve the exact numeric string from the selected net amount cell.
- Do not invent extra funds, extra slots, or separate same-day direction rows that are not directly supported by the document.

Output discipline
- Downstream saving for this counterparty uses only_pending=true, but the extraction stage must first produce the full internal order set.
