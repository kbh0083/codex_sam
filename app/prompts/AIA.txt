- "Buy / Amount" and "Sell / Amount" may both contain numeric values in the same row.
- Treat Buy as SUB and Sell as RED, and keep them as separate orders unless a dedicated net settlement column is explicitly present.
- Use the exact numeric amount from the Buy or Sell amount cell as transfer_amount evidence.
- Ignore manager-identification columns such as "External Fund Manager" once the row itself has been identified.
- Rows with "-", blank, or zero in the Buy or Sell amount cell are not transactions for that direction.
- Emit orders only from rows whose fund identity and Buy/Sell amount cell are both explicitly visible.
- Do not invent extra funds, duplicate mirrored rows, or additional buckets that are not printed in the report.
